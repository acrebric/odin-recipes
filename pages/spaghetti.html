Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a simple Italian pasta. It requires few ingredients to prepare.

To assist you in your journey crafting this delicious dish, there will be all prerequisites, ingredients, and steps listed below.

Information

    • Cook Time: 15 Minutes
    • Prep Time: 10 mins
    • Servings: 4

Ingredients

    • Spaghetti: This recipe will require one pound of uncooked spaghetti.
    • Oil: You’ll need ½ cup of olive oil.
    • Garlic: Six cloves of thinly sliced garlic lends bold flavor.
    • Seasonings: This traditional pasta recipe will utilize the flavor of red pepper flakes, black pepper, and fresh parsley.
    • Cheese: For optimal results, use some Parmesean and/or Romano cheese,

How to Prepare Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

    1. Boil the spaghetti.
    2. Toast the garlic in olive oil.
    3. Stir the seasonings into the pasta, then stir in the garlic and oil
    4. Toss with cheese.

How can I store Spaghetti Aglio e Olio?

Place the meal inside an airtight container. Place the container in your fridge for safe keeping.
